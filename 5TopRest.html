These are my top 5 restaurants in the Tempe area!

  • Gen, Korean BBQ

    Gen Korean BBQ is one of the best places in Tempe for authentic Korean BBQ. You can cook it in front of you and your friends. It's a great time and a great atmosphere for groups or couples.

  • Thirsty Lion Gastropub

    The Thirsty Lion Gastropub has the best music playing as you're eating. They even play cool music videos. Their drinks are innovative and it's a good time for those who want a relaxing place to eat and a good bar.

  • Olive Garden

    Olive Garden has been an American staple for a long time. This has been one of my favorite restaurants since I was a child. Perhaps it's the nostalgia of it, however I still really like their food. It's basic, but it's good comfort food.

  • Red Lobster

    Red Lobster took me some time to get into, since I don't have an affinity for seafood. As I got older I started to appreciate it more. Their crab and lobster combo is some of the best, even better than really expensive restaurants.

  • Kabuki

    Kabuki is a simple but stylish japanese restaurant in Tempe Marketplace. It offers many staple Japanese foods for anyone to eat. The dessert is also the best part of the entire place. The vibe of the restaurant is good as well, having many homages to Japan.
